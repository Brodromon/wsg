One of the main ideas of the University of Economics are the merger and integration, as well as the exchange of experience that has accumulated over 25 years of existence of the University in the Polish market.

For many years in a row, we have been actively working with our international partners to create innovative structures through which the quality of our educational products remains at the highest European level.

The Nicolaus Copernicus European Institute is another innovative initiative that expands the horizons of European cooperation, not only academic, but also economic, cultural and social.
