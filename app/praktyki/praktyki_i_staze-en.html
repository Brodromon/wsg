Practices and internships

The Internship Office of the University of Economy cooperates with many enterprises in which all students of our University have the opportunity to take up apprenticeships. In the wide range of the Internship Bureau, each student will find practices appropriate to his teaching and, of course, professional interests.

Students have the opportunity to practice internationally also. Long-term cooperation with hotels in Greece, Portugal, Spain, France and others. It contributed to the fact that every year a large number of students take advantage of foreign internships.

For anyone who wants to increase their opportunity on the labor market, we offer internships. Students after completing internships are more attractive to a potential employer because they already have professional experience. Internships and foreign internships, which the University of Economics has in its offer, opens many doors to graduates of our University.
